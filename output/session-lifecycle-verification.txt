2. hooks.rs — HookEvent::Stop variant + HookRunner::run_stop()

       working_dir, task_started_at, prompt, is_followup
   - run_stop_hook() delegates to hook_runner.run_stop(session_id)

   - Called before REPL loop in run_repl()
   - LiveCli::run_stop_hook() calls self.runtime.run_stop_hook()
   - Wired at both exit points: /exit|/quit and ReadOutcome::Exit
